## Ingredients

* 300g greek yogurt
* 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
* 10g mint leaves, finely chopped
* 2 tablespoons lemon juice
* 1 garlic clove
* Salt and pepper to taste

> Note: Adjust seasonings to taste
