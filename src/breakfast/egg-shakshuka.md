# Shakshuka Eggs

## Recipe 1

```
Finely chopped onion and butter
Chopped green chillies and Ginger
Chopped Tomatoes
Turmeric, Red chilli powder, Pav bhaji masala

Break eggs with pinch of salt and whisk
Mix nicely and evenly on a slow flame
Fresh coriander
```
