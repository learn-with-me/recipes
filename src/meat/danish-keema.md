# Keema

## Ingredients

* Ground beef
* Onion
* Ginger
* Garlic
* Salt
* Haldi - turmeric
* Red Chili ground

## Procedure

* Prepare masala
    * Onion to be cut fine-long
    * Ginger-Garlic paste
* Place keema and masala
* Add Salt, Turmeric, Red chili powder
