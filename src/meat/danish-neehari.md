# Nihari

* Heat oil
* Add nihari masala (30 seconds)
* Ginger-Garlic paste
* Meat
* Water 1-ltr
* Salt
* 40 mins. High pressure-High Temperature
* Separate oil
* 100gm Maida or all purpose flour in 200ml cold water - make sure mix in high temp
* Heat ON
* Green chilli, Ginger
