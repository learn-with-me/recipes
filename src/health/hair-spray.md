# Hair spray

## Highlights

| | |
|----|-----|
| Prep Time             | 10 min      |
| Cook Time             | 6 hours     |
| Total Time            | 6 hours     |

## Ingredients

* 1 tablespoon dried rosemary leaves
* 1 teaspoon cloves
* 12 oz water
* Optional
    * 1 cinnamon stick

## Process

* Infuse ingredients in warm water overnight.
* Strain the mixture and pour into a spray bottle
* Use this daily for maximum results

> Note: for crazy hair growth
